Our Story

Authentic Italian Heritage

Nestled in the heart of Historic Downtown District, Bella Luna Ristorante brings you four generations of authentic Tuscan recipes passed down through the Benedetti family.

Our intimate 50-seat dining room creates the perfect atmosphere for special occasions, romantic dinners, and unforgettable culinary experiences. Every dish is crafted with locally-sourced ingredients from our 20+ year partnerships with regional farms.

From our handmade pasta prepared fresh daily to our award-winning wine cellar featuring over 200 selections, we honor tradition while embracing culinary innovation.

Chef Marco Benedetti

Master of Tuscan Cuisine

Executive Chef & Owner

Trained in the renowned kitchens of Florence and mentored by Michelin-starred chefs, Chef Marco brings authentic Italian techniques combined with innovative modern presentations.

His passion for sustainable sourcing and farm-to-table practices has earned Bella Luna recognition as a leader in conscious dining. Every dish tells a story of tradition, craftsmanship, and respect for ingredients.
