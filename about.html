My Story: From Code to Community

I'm a software engineer who went to school right here in Fresno and now work for our local power utility, serving the customers of my hometown. Every day, I see how technology can solve real problems for real people.

But I also watched too many local business owners - people I know, neighbors I grew up with - get burned by big agencies that took their money and disappeared. Long contracts, vanity metrics, and zero accountability. It broke my heart.

That's when I realized: I could use my technical background to give back to my community. Not as another faceless agency, but as a local guy who genuinely cares about seeing our businesses thrive.

I don't need to scale to thousands of clients or build an empire. I just want to help the hardworking business owners in my area finally get social media that actually generates leads and drives revenue. Real results from someone they can trust - not a corporate machine.
