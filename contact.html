Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A standard website typically takes 4-8 weeks, while comprehensive branding projects may take 6-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your web design service?

Our web design service includes custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, content management system integration, analytics setup, and 3 months of post-launch support.

Do you work with businesses outside Mountain View?

Absolutely! While we're based in Mountain View, we work with clients nationwide. Most of our collaboration happens virtually, and we're experienced in remote project management.

What's your payment structure?

We typically work with a 50% deposit to begin the project and 50% upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. All pricing is transparent with no hidden fees.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We include 3 months of complimentary support with every project. After that, we offer monthly maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally.

Can you help improve my existing website?

Definitely! We offer website audits, redesigns, and optimization services. Whether you need a complete overhaul or specific improvements, we can help enhance your existing digital presence.
